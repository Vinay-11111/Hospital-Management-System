our services

Lab Service

We strive to maintain the highest standards of quality in all aspects of care, and meet the needs of our patients promptly and professionally with a friendly, caring attitude, with a personal approach. The Labaratory will send your test results anywhere needed all over the country. We strive to provide cost effective and accurate test results in a timely manner.

24-hour Laboratory Service

The Wayne County Hospital Laboratory is a 24 hour 7 day a week CAP (College of American Pathology) accredited laboratory fully staffed by ASCP certified technicians and technologists. The Laboratory offers adult and pediatric blood drawing services.

Testing Procedures

A written physician, ARNP, or PA order is required for outpatient testing to be performed. Laboratory results assists in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases by performing tests on blood and other body fluids. All results will be faxed to the ordering physician or facility once completed.

Examples of Services Offered

  • Hematology/coagulationstudies
  • Routine chemistry as well as specially immunodiagnostic testing
  • Blood bank: blood type, antibody screenings, and crossmatch
  • Microbiology: cultures and sensitivies
  • Urinalysis/serology
