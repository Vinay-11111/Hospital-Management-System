about us

The Hospital is a 25-bed Critical Access Hospital in Monticello, Kentucky. It is located about 30 minutes southwest of Somerset, Kentucky and about 5-8 miles from the shores of Lake Cumberland. Wayne County Hospital serves a region which includes residents of Wayne County, Pulaski County, McCreary County and Clinton County in Kentucky, and portions of Northern Tennessee on our southern border. The Wayne County Hospital was built in 1971 and redesigned in 1996 and 2003. Wayne County Hospital is fully accredited by the Healthcare Facilities Accreditation Program (HFAP) and has a number of programs to meet the needs of its populations.

Wayne County Hospital is proud of its swing bed (extended recovery and rehabilitation) program which allows us to serve many orthopedic and medical patients requiring longer recovery and rehab services. As we’ve grown to meet the ever changing needs of our population we continue to provide the same high quality, compassionate care you’ve come to expect which is close to home.
