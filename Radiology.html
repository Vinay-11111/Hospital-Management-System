our services

radiology

Our radiology department offers a complete line of radiology services to meet your needs. We have 3 radiologists on staff as well as an after hours radiology service.

All your exams will be read the same day and can be read quickly in STAT situations. All final reports are available 1-2 days after readings. Our radiologists are board certified and have many years of experience.



Our experienced staff brings a hometown, friendly atmosphere to the hospital, and will always try to accommodate your schedule to get you the quickest reading possible. We have state-of-the-art equipment that is state certified and physicist tested annually.

The newest additions and services to our department include our 16 slice Toshiba CT scanner, which provides precise low dose imaging. We now also offer Low Dose lung cancer screenings.

We have a mobile MRI unit that has the highest Tesla (magnet strength) in the county. The MRI is at Wayne County Hospital on Thursdays. This closed MRI unit is designed for precise imaging and will provide the images needed for many advanced diagnostic needs. In most instances, you will not have to worry about having your MRI repeated, as is often the case with other low field-strength open MRIs in our area.

We are here to provide you with excellent, professional care for all your radiology needs. Call us to schedule an appointment or with any questions 606-340-3236.
